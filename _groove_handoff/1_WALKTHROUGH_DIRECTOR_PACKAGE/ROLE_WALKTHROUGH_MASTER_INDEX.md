# Role Walkthrough Master Index

This index covers all eight role-based walkthroughs for the internal Loan Factory LO Development Platform staging build, plus the global screen recording checklist. Use this document to plan your recording session and confirm coverage of every approved Loan Factory user role.

**Platform under test:** https://loan-factory-elite-sales-marketing-tr.netlify.app
**Auth:** Google OAuth via Supabase, restricted to @loanfactory.com email addresses
**Master Admin recorder:** Jeremy McDonald (jeremy.mcdonald@loanfactory.com)
**View-As tool:** `/admin/view-as` (master admin only) with an orange banner shown while active

## Recommended Recording Order

Record in this order so each clip builds on the prior one and the recorder only needs to enter View-As once per role:

1. Master Admin (signed in directly)
2. Normal LO (via View-As)
3. LO Development (via View-As, Andre King's role)

## Walkthrough Summaries

### 1. Master Admin Walkthrough
Demonstrates the highest-permission tour: admin shell, the user table at `/admin/users` with role and department badges, AI Assistants admin controls, and the View-As launcher. Confirms only Jeremy McDonald sees the master admin controls.
- File: `master_admin_walkthrough.md`
- Estimated runtime: 6–8 minutes

### 2. Normal LO Walkthrough
The baseline approved Loan Factory loan officer experience: home dashboard, AI Assistants hub, profile edit, Facegram feed, member-area entry points, and the lender escalation form. This is the most common role on the platform.
- File: `normal_lo_walkthrough.md`
- Estimated runtime: 5–7 minutes

### 3. LO Development Walkthrough
Andre King's lens as LO Development Lead: the full sales-training stack (101 through 601), AI training, scripts, trackers, and the One Plus One Five module set. Highlights the curriculum surface area Andre owns.
- File: `lo_development_walkthrough.md`
- Estimated runtime: 6–8 minutes

### 4. Corporate Coach Walkthrough
Coaching workspace tour: `/coaching`, `/apex-member-area`, `/coach-guide`, and `/coach-reports`. Shows how a coach reviews assigned LOs and plans sessions.
- File: `corporate_coach_walkthrough.md`
- Estimated runtime: 5–7 minutes

### 5. Marketing Walkthrough
Content surface walkthrough: `/401-content-marketing`, the recordings library, prompts library, audio training, and the AI training video library. Frames the marketing role as the content operator on the platform.
- File: `marketing_walkthrough.md`
- Estimated runtime: 5–7 minutes

### 6. Loan Officer Support Walkthrough
Support routing tour: `/support-routing`, `/lender-escalation`, `/resources`, plus the user lookup affordances available to support staff. Shows the inbound triage path.
- File: `loan_officer_support_walkthrough.md`
- Estimated runtime: 4–6 minutes

### 7. LO Mastery 249 Walkthrough
Member tier walkthrough for the $249 LO Mastery program inside `/member-area/lo-mastery`. Confirms gated content visibility for paid members.
- File: `lo_mastery_249_walkthrough.md`
- Estimated runtime: 4–6 minutes

### 8. Alliance 449 Walkthrough
Member tier walkthrough for the $449 Alliance program inside `/member-area/alliance`. Highlights the elite execution surface and the higher-tier deliverables versus LO Mastery.
- File: `alliance_449_walkthrough.md`
- Estimated runtime: 4–6 minutes
