# Marketing Walkthrough

**Audience:** Loan Factory marketing department and leadership reviewing the content operator surface.

**Goal:** Tour the content surfaces a marketing user touches: `/401-content-marketing`, `/recordings`, `/prompts`, `/audio-training`, and `/ai-training/video-library`.

**Starting route:** `/admin/view-as` (master admin selects the Marketing role, then lands on `/`)

**Estimated runtime:** 5–7 minutes

**Suggested intro script:** "This is the Marketing view of the LO Development Platform. I'm previewing it through View-As so you can see the content libraries a marketing user works from."

## Exact steps

1. From `/admin/view-as`, pick the Marketing role, confirm the orange banner, and land on `/`.
2. Open `/401-content-marketing` and walk the curriculum and asset cards.
3. Navigate to `/recordings` and scroll the library briefly.
4. Open `/prompts` and call out the prompt categories.
5. Open `/audio-training` and show the audio companion cards.
6. Open `/ai-training/video-library` and scroll the embeds.
7. Return to `/` to show the home dashboard from the marketing lens.
8. Click **Exit View-As**. Land URL: `/admin/view-as`.

## What to click

- The content marketing tile on `/sales-training` or the direct `/401-content-marketing` link.
- A sample recording card on `/recordings` (do not play audio in the clip).
- A prompt category chip on `/prompts`.

## What success looks like

- Viewer can see all five marketing surfaces load: `/401-content-marketing`, `/recordings`, `/prompts`, `/audio-training`, `/ai-training/video-library`.
- Viewer can see the orange View-As banner throughout.
- Viewer can identify where a marketer pulls scripts, prompts, audio, and video assets.
- Viewer can confirm admin tools are not in the nav for this role.

## Common mistakes

- Auto-playing audio or video. Hover but do not click play.
- Skipping `/prompts` because it looks like a small library — it is core to the marketing role.
- Recording while a search field has stale text. Clear search inputs before each route.

## Recording notes

- Mute the browser tab to avoid surprise audio playback.
- The orange View-As banner must remain visible.
- Anonymize any LO names that appear as content authors.
- Keep the closing frame on `/ai-training/video-library` if possible to leave the viewer on the richest asset surface.

**Suggested closing script:** "That is the Marketing content surface. Exiting View-As and moving to Loan Officer Support."
