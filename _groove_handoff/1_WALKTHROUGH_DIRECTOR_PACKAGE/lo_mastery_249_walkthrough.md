# LO Mastery 249 Walkthrough

**Audience:** Approved Loan Factory loan officers enrolled in the $249 LO Mastery program, plus leadership confirming gated content visibility.

**Goal:** Show the $249 LO Mastery member experience inside `/member-area/lo-mastery`, confirm gated content is unlocked for paid members, and contrast it with the Normal LO baseline.

**Starting route:** `/admin/view-as` (master admin selects the LO Mastery 249 member role, then lands on `/`)

**Estimated runtime:** 4–6 minutes

**Suggested intro script:** "This is the LO Mastery 249 member view. I'm previewing it through View-As so you can confirm a $249 member sees the gated content unlocked."

## Exact steps

1. From `/admin/view-as`, pick the LO Mastery 249 member role, confirm the orange banner, and land on `/`.
2. Click into `/member-area` and call out which program tiles are unlocked versus locked.
3. Open `/member-area/lo-mastery` and walk the unlocked curriculum, recordings, and resource sections.
4. Open `/ai-assistants/` briefly to confirm the standard LO AI surfaces still load for this member.
5. Return to `/member-area/lo-mastery` so the closing frame is on the member curriculum.
6. Click **Exit View-As**. Land URL: `/admin/view-as`.

## What to click

- The Member Area tile on `/`.
- The LO Mastery program card on `/member-area`.
- One unlocked lesson card inside `/member-area/lo-mastery` to demonstrate access.

## What success looks like

- Viewer can see `/member-area/lo-mastery` loads with content unlocked, not gated.
- Viewer can see the Alliance tier is still visible but appears locked or marked as a separate tier.
- Viewer can see the orange View-As banner throughout.
- Viewer can confirm the AI Assistants hub is still reachable for this member.

## Common mistakes

- Recording from a member account whose paid flag is not seeded — the gate will block content and the demo will fail. Pick a seeded LO Mastery 249 member in View-As.
- Confusing LO Mastery with Alliance. Stay on `/member-area/lo-mastery` for this clip.
- Playing audio or video inside a lesson. Hover and scroll only.

## Recording notes

- The orange View-As banner must remain visible.
- Use seeded demo member data only.
- Keep the cursor on the LO Mastery card on `/member-area` for a beat so the viewer can see the tier label.

**Suggested closing script:** "That is the LO Mastery 249 member experience. Exiting View-As and moving to the Alliance 449 view next."
