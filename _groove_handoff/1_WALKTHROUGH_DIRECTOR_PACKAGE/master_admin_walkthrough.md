# Master Admin Walkthrough

**Audience:** Loan Factory leadership and internal admins who need to see the full master admin control surface (Jeremy McDonald's view).

**Goal:** Show that the master admin account can reach every administrative tool, review the seeded approved Loan Factory user table, manage AI assistant defaults, and launch View-As to preview any other role.

**Starting route:** `/`

**Estimated runtime:** 6–8 minutes

**Suggested intro script:** "This is the master admin tour of the internal Loan Factory LO Development Platform. I'm signed in as Jeremy McDonald and I'll walk through the admin shell, the approved user table, AI assistant controls, and View-As."

## Exact steps

1. Land on `/` and point out the home dashboard cards and the master admin nav entry. Land URL: `/`.
2. Click the **Admin** nav link to open `/admin`. Highlight the status panel and the admin tool tiles.
3. Click **Users** (or navigate to `/admin/users`). Scroll the table and call out role badges, department chips, and multi-role indicators on the 35 seeded approved Loan Factory users.
4. Open `/admin/ai-assistants`. Walk through the AI Twin default toggles and explain that these are organization-wide defaults.
5. Return to `/admin` and click **View-As** to land on `/admin/view-as`. Show the role picker but do not enter View-As during this clip.
6. Open `/profile` to confirm the master admin profile renders, then `/profile/edit` to show the edit form briefly.
7. End on `/admin` so the closing frame shows the admin shell.

## What to click

- The **Admin** entry in the top nav (only the master admin sees it).
- The **Users** tile or sidebar link inside `/admin`.
- The **AI Assistants** admin tile (not the public `/ai-assistants` hub).
- The **View-As** launcher button on `/admin` or the direct `/admin/view-as` route.

## What success looks like

- Viewer can see that `/admin`, `/admin/users`, `/admin/ai-assistants`, and `/admin/view-as` all load without error for Jeremy.
- Viewer can identify role and department badges on the approved Loan Factory user table.
- Viewer can locate the View-As entry point and understand it is master admin only.
- Viewer can see the AI Assistants admin controls separate from the `/ai-assistants` hub.

## Common mistakes

- Recording while logged in as a non-master account. Confirm Jeremy's avatar in the top right before pressing record.
- Forgetting to scroll the user table — the multi-role badges only show on certain rows.
- Clicking into View-As during this clip. View-As belongs to each individual role walkthrough.
- Showing real borrower data or PII in any screenshot.

## Recording notes

- This walkthrough is recorded **without** View-As. The orange banner must not appear.
- If the orange banner is visible, click **Exit View-As** before starting.
- Anonymize any visible loan officer phone numbers or non-Loan Factory email addresses with on-screen blur or by scrolling past them.
- Keep the browser window at 1920x1080 with bookmarks hidden.

**Suggested closing script:** "That's the master admin surface. In the next clips I'll use View-As from this same screen to preview every other approved Loan Factory role without signing out."
