# Alliance 449 Walkthrough

**Audience:** Approved Loan Factory loan officers enrolled in the $449 Alliance program, plus leadership confirming the higher-tier deliverables versus LO Mastery.

**Goal:** Show the $449 Alliance member experience inside `/member-area/alliance`, highlight the advanced coaching deliverables, and contrast it with the LO Mastery 249 tier.

**Starting route:** `/admin/view-as` (master admin selects the Alliance 449 member role, then lands on `/`)

**Estimated runtime:** 4–6 minutes

**Suggested intro script:** "This is the Alliance 449 member view. I'm previewing it through View-As so you can confirm the Alliance tier sees the advanced coaching deliverables unlocked."

## Exact steps

1. From `/admin/view-as`, pick the Alliance 449 member role, confirm the orange banner, and land on `/`.
2. Click into `/member-area` and confirm both LO Mastery and Alliance tiles read as unlocked.
3. Open `/member-area/alliance` and walk the Alliance deliverables, sessions, and resource sections.
4. Open `/601-elite-execution` only as a free 101-601 training reference, not as Alliance paid progress.
5. Open `/ai-assistants/` briefly to confirm the standard AI surfaces still load.
6. Return to `/member-area/alliance` so the closing frame is on the Alliance hub.
7. Click **Exit View-As**. Land URL: `/admin/view-as`.

## What to click

- The Member Area tile on `/`.
- The Alliance program card on `/member-area`.
- An Alliance deliverable card inside `/member-area/alliance` to demonstrate access.

## What success looks like

- Viewer can see `/member-area/alliance` loads with Alliance content unlocked.
- Viewer can see `/601-elite-execution` loads as a separate free training reference.
- Viewer can see the orange View-As banner throughout.
- Viewer can visually contrast Alliance with LO Mastery so the tier difference is obvious.

## Common mistakes

- Recording from a member account whose Alliance flag is not seeded — the gate will block content. Pick a seeded Alliance 449 member in View-As.
- Treating `/601-elite-execution` as paid Alliance progress. It is a free training reference.
- Confusing Alliance with LO Mastery. Stay on the Alliance routes for this clip.

## Recording notes

- The orange View-As banner must remain visible.
- Use seeded demo member data only.
- Hover over the Alliance tile on `/member-area` for a beat so the viewer can see the tier label and price.

**Suggested closing script:** "That is the Alliance 449 member experience. Exiting View-As — that wraps the role tour."
