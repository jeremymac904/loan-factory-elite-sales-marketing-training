# Normal LO Walkthrough

**Audience:** Approved Loan Factory loan officers using the platform day to day, plus leadership reviewing the baseline LO experience.

**Goal:** Show the default loan officer surface: home dashboard, AI Assistants hub, profile, Facegram, member area entry, and the lender escalation form.

**Starting route:** `/admin/view-as` (master admin selects the Normal LO role, then lands on `/`)

**Estimated runtime:** 5–7 minutes

**Suggested intro script:** "This is the standard approved Loan Factory loan officer view. I'm previewing the Normal LO role through View-As so you can see exactly what a frontline loan officer sees when they sign in."

## Exact steps

1. From `/admin/view-as`, select the Normal LO role and confirm the orange View-As banner appears, then land on `/`.
2. Walk the home dashboard cards on `/` and call out the AI Assistants, Facegram, and Member Area entry tiles.
3. Click into `/ai-assistants/` and show the hub layout, then open `/ai-assistants/my-ai-twin` for a few seconds.
4. Navigate to `/profile`, then `/profile/edit`, and demonstrate that the edit form is usable for a Normal LO.
5. Open `/facegram` and scroll the feed briefly, then peek at `/facegram/notifications`.
6. Navigate to `/member-area` to show the program tiles and confirm gating cues for tiers the LO is not enrolled in.
7. Open `/lender-escalation` and walk the form fields without submitting.
8. Click **Exit View-As** in the orange banner. Land URL: `/admin/view-as`.

## What to click

- The View-As role picker entry for the Normal LO role on `/admin/view-as`.
- The AI Assistants tile on `/` and the My AI Twin link inside the hub.
- The **Edit profile** button on `/profile`.
- The orange **Exit View-As** button at the very end.

## What success looks like

- Viewer can see the orange View-As banner the entire time.
- Viewer can identify the five main pillars a Normal LO touches: AI Assistants, Profile, Facegram, Member Area, Lender Escalation.
- Viewer can see that admin-only routes (`/admin`, `/admin/users`) are not surfaced in the nav while in this role.
- Viewer can confirm the lender escalation form loads and is fillable.

## Common mistakes

- Forgetting to engage View-As, which would record the master admin nav instead of the LO nav.
- Submitting the lender escalation form. Walk it visually only.
- Showing a real borrower name or loan number on screen.
- Skipping the **Exit View-As** click at the end of the clip.

## Recording notes

- The orange banner must be visible on every frame after step 1.
- Use seeded demo data; do not type real client info into the escalation form.
- If Facegram shows a real LO photo, scroll past it quickly or pick a seeded demo post.
- Keep cursor movement deliberate so the viewer can follow.

**Suggested closing script:** "That's the Normal LO experience. I'm exiting View-As now and the next clip will preview the LO Development role."
