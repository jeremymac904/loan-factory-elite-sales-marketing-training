# LO Development Walkthrough

**Audience:** Andre King (LO Development Lead), Loan Factory leadership, and the LO Development department staff.

**Goal:** Tour the LO Development curriculum surface: the 101 through 601 sales training stack, AI training, One Plus One Five, scripts, trackers, recordings, and the training library.

**Starting route:** `/admin/view-as` (master admin selects the LO Development role, then lands on `/`)

**Estimated runtime:** 6–8 minutes

**Suggested intro script:** "This is the LO Development view, the role Andre King uses to manage curriculum. I'm previewing it through View-As so you can see the full training surface area at a glance."

## Exact steps

1. From `/admin/view-as`, pick the LO Development role and confirm the orange banner, then land on `/`.
2. Click into `/sales-training` and point out the six-course ladder.
3. Walk through the sales training URLs in sequence: `/101-foundation`, `/201-borrower-conversion`, `/301-referral-partner-growth`, `/401-content-marketing`, `/501-pipeline-sales-systems`, `/601-elite-execution`. Spend a few seconds on each landing page.
4. Open `/ai-training` and then `/ai-training/video-library` to show the AI training video library.
5. Navigate to `/one-plus-one-five` and call out that there are nine sub-routes. Click into two of them as samples.
6. Open `/scripts`, then `/trackers`, then `/recordings` to show the operational toolkit.
7. End on `/training-library`.
8. Click **Exit View-As**. Land URL: `/admin/view-as`.

## What to click

- The sales training tile on `/` or the direct `/sales-training` link.
- Each course card on `/sales-training` in numeric order.
- The video library link inside `/ai-training`.
- The One Plus One Five hub tile and a couple of its sub-route cards.

## What success looks like

- Viewer can see every sales training URL from 101 through 601 load successfully.
- Viewer can see the AI training hub and its video library route.
- Viewer can see One Plus One Five and at least two of its nine sub-routes.
- Viewer can see the operational toolkit (`/scripts`, `/trackers`, `/recordings`, `/training-library`) is reachable from this role.

## Common mistakes

- Spending too long on one course page. Aim for 8–12 seconds per landing page.
- Forgetting to click into a couple of One Plus One Five sub-routes — the hub alone does not show the depth.
- Showing the admin nav. Confirm the orange banner first.
- Recording while the AI training video library is auto-playing audio.

## Recording notes

- Mute any auto-playing video preview before recording.
- The orange View-As banner must remain visible throughout.
- If a sales training page shows a real LO name as the author, scroll past the byline.
- Anonymize any LO photos visible in recordings or training cards.

**Suggested closing script:** "That is the LO Development curriculum stack. I'll exit View-As and move to the Corporate Coach role next."
