## Post 8 — Lender Escalation Tip

- **Author:** Benjamin
- **Group:** Main feed
- **Post type:** Lender escalation tip
- **Hook:** "Save your future self the headache."

```
Tip on lender escalations — when you fill out the form inside the platform, lead with the deadline first, not the backstory.

What I've seen work:
- Line 1: "Need a response by [DATE] or this deal moves."
- Line 2–3: What's stuck, in one breath.
- Line 4: What you've already tried.
- Line 5: What you need (call, doc, exception, etc.).

The form gets read faster when the deadline is the first thing the reviewer sees. Save your future self the headache.

— Benjamin
```
