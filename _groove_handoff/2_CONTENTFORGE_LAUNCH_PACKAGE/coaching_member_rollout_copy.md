## Coach Intro Template — When a Coach Hands a New Member Their Tier Login

**Use case:** A coach has just onboarded a new coaching member and needs to send the welcome message that gets them into the right tier home on day one.

**Channel:** Direct email or DM from coach to new member.

**Approval:** Coach owns the send. Jeremy approves the template once.

```
Subject: You're in — your [LO Mastery / Alliance] tier is set up

Hey [FIRST NAME],

You're set up on the [LO Mastery / Alliance] tier inside the Loan Factory LO Development platform.

Sign in here: https://loan-factory-elite-sales-marketing-tr.netlify.app
Your tier home is in your left nav — [/coaching/lo-mastery OR /coaching/alliance].

Three things to do in your first week:

1. Show up to [Power Hour at 9:00 AM PT / Breakfast Club at the time listed in your tier page] tomorrow. Just show up — you don't need to bring anything the first day.

2. Open your tier page and skim the resources index. Pick two things you'll actually use this week and bookmark them with the Save button (they'll land in /facegram/saved).

3. Book our next 1:1. [Link or instructions for booking with this specific coach.]

Your [LO Mastery / Alliance] Coaching Assistant is in your dashboard under /ai-assistants. Drafts only — you send. Use it to prep for our next session.

Questions on access, tier, or billing — reply to me. Anything platform-level, go to Jeremy.

Welcome in.

— [COACH NAME]
[COACH TITLE]
```
