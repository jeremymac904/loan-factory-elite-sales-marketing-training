## Rules (apply to every deliverable in the first wave)

- **Public sources only.** No scraping behind logins. No screenshots of internal CRMs.
- **No private comp data.** Use only what is publicly published on company sites, press releases, public coverage (HousingWire, NMP, Glassdoor public ranges only as one of multiple sources).
- **Cite every source URL + date_checked.** A row without a citation is not "collected" — it is framework only.
- **Flag "incomplete advertised pricing"** when fees / APR / comp grid / E&O / per-file fees are missing from public pages.
- **Never plagiarize.** Paraphrase, attribute, adapt for internal-only use.
- **Internal-only distribution.** Do not republish these one-pagers externally; they are recruiting-conversation and product-strategy support.
