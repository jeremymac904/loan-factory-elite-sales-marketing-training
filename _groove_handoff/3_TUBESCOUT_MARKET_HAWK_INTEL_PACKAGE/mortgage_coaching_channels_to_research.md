# Mortgage Coaching Channels to Research

**Purpose:** Named channels for the TubeScout researcher to mine for: scripts, cadence patterns, leaderboard formats, accountability language, tracker examples, and Power Hour mechanics. We are sourcing **inspiration** for the $249 LO Mastery + $449 Alliance tiers — we never copy scripts verbatim.

**Verification source:** Web searches run 2026-05-28 (`top mortgage loan officer coaching programs 2026 pricing`, `best YouTube channels mortgage loan officer training 2026`, `Mortgage Marketing Animals Carl White Loan Officer Freedom coaching`).

**Capture rule:** Every video reviewed -> one row in `youtube_research_capture_template.csv`. Mark `adapt_to` field; never adapt to public-facing material — internal only.

---

## Channels (10+)

### 1. Mortgage Marketing Animals / Loan Officer Freedom (Carl White)
- **Focus:** Daily Success Plan, 32-hour work week framing, scripts, accountability, Realtor referral systems, Freedom Club mastermind.
- **Why it matters:** Most-cited mortgage coaching brand. Their identity-first framing ("loan officer freedom") competes directly with our Alliance tier value prop.
- **What to harvest:** Daily success cadence, Realtor partner script structure (paraphrased), call reluctance reframes, accountability ritual language. Search query used: `Mortgage Marketing Animals Carl White Loan Officer Freedom coaching`.

### 2. MortgageCoach (Dave Savage / Total Cost Analysis)
- **Focus:** Borrower education as competitive advantage; TCA tool walkthroughs; cost-of-waiting framing; Realtor partner workflows.
- **Why it matters:** Defines the "educator" archetype LOs aspire to. Their pitch is "be the expert" — directly informs how we position AI Twin as an expert-in-a-box for LO Mastery members.
- **What to harvest:** Borrower education framing, cost-of-waiting math language, Realtor handoff scripts.

### 3. The Mortgage Calculator — Loan Officer Training Series Podcast
- **Focus:** Long-form training episodes for new and mid-career LOs.
- **Why it matters:** High-volume episodic content — useful for sampling current pain points in the market.
- **What to harvest:** Pain-point list for AI Advantage micro-lesson backlog; cadence model for our own video library.

### 4. TheReSourceTV (Ryan Hills + RC Christensen)
- **Focus:** Weekly mortgage + real estate news, training, entertainment value.
- **Why it matters:** Sets the tone bar for "fun and informative." Useful template for FaceGram weekly digest tone.
- **What to harvest:** Hook structures, segment transitions, audience interaction patterns.

### 5. Your Mortgage Millions Mentor
- **Focus:** Doubling income, working fewer hours, no-cold-call positioning.
- **Why it matters:** Direct competitor positioning to Alliance tier — they sell "freedom" too.
- **What to harvest:** Pricing/offer page structure, no-cold-call framing, work-less language patterns.

### 6. Iron Point Mortgage (Kevin)
- **Focus:** Lifestyle + mortgage business hybrid, fun framing.
- **Why it matters:** Strong personal-brand template — useful for FaceGram personality-driven seed content.
- **What to harvest:** Lifestyle-business blend ratio, hook openers, on-camera ease patterns.

### 7. Fairway Independent — Nicole's Channel
- **Focus:** Binge-worthy playlists for consumers, agents, LOs.
- **Why it matters:** Playlist architecture is best-in-class. Informs how we structure AI Advantage video library navigation.
- **What to harvest:** Playlist titling conventions, thumbnail consistency, audience-segment-per-playlist model.

### 8. TLOPonline (Dustin Owen)
- **Focus:** Sales and practical training for loan officers.
- **Why it matters:** Practical script library — informs what kind of scripts our Scripts library should contain.
- **What to harvest:** Script topic list (not the scripts themselves), training-segment lengths, follow-up framing.

### 9. The Core Training (Rick Ruby) — YouTube + public content
- **Focus:** Top-producer coaching, branch manager training, leadership cadence.
- **Why it matters:** Premium-tier coaching pricing comparable to Alliance. Their leadership-tier framing maps to our Team Leader Playbook.
- **What to harvest:** Branch manager weekly cadence, leadership KPI language, team accountability rituals.

### 10. Mortgage Champions (Dale Vermillion)
- **Focus:** Veteran trainer; classic mortgage sales fundamentals; objection handling.
- **Why it matters:** Fundamentals layer of LO Mastery curriculum. Anchors "timeless" content vs. trendy tactics.
- **What to harvest:** Objection-handling categories, fundamentals checklist structure, training-progression ladder.

### 11. High Trust Coaching (Todd Duncan)
- **Focus:** Producer-level coaching, business discipline, performance dashboard tracking.
- **Why it matters:** Premium-pricing benchmark ($700/mo to $30K/yr per public reports). Anchors top of our pricing comparison.
- **What to harvest:** Dashboard KPI taxonomy, bi-weekly call cadence, accountability format.

### 12. 20/20 Vision for Success (Christine Beckwith)
- **Focus:** Leadership + production coaching, often women-in-mortgage focused.
- **Why it matters:** Differentiated audience cut — informs whether Loan Factory needs a leadership-focused track inside Alliance.
- **What to harvest:** Leadership-track topic list, audience-segmentation language, mentorship model.

### 13. Good Vibe Squad
- **Focus:** High-performance coaching, lead-gen integration.
- **Why it matters:** They bundle coaching + leads — useful contrast since LF bundles coaching + AI + lender escalation.
- **What to harvest:** Bundle structure, value-stack language, pricing-page architecture.

### 14. Knowledge Coop (Coop+)
- **Focus:** Subscription LMS for CE/PE + deeper mortgage training.
- **Why it matters:** Subscription LMS pricing reference (~$30/mo per public reports). Anchors bottom of pricing comparison vs. our $249/$449 tiers.
- **What to harvest:** LMS curriculum taxonomy, CE/PE bundle framing, subscription onboarding flow.

- 1 "adapt_to: AI Advantage micro-lesson" note per channel where AI angle exists.
