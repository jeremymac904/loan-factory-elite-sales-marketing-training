## Capture Targets per Channel

- 2-3 evergreen clips captured per channel in first wave.
- 1 "adapt_to: FaceGram seed" per channel.
- 1 "adapt_to: Coaching session opener" per channel where the identity framing fits.
- Flag any channel whose tone clashes with Loan Factory ("vet for tone fit") so future researchers know to slow down.

## Tone Filter

Before capturing any clip into the CSV, the researcher must answer:

1. Would this survive a compliance review in an internal-only Loan Factory training context? (Y/N)
2. Does the identity language assume "we sell anyone anything" or "we serve borrowers well"? (Must be the second.)
3. Is the framing evergreen (24+ months) or tied to a 2026 news cycle?

If any answer fails, skip the clip.
