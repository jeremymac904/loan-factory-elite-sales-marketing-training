# Competitor Research Targets — Market Hawk

**Purpose:** Named competitor targets across 5 segments. Each target gets a row in `competitor_intel_capture_template.csv` and (where it owns a platform) a row in `platform_feature_comparison_template.csv`. Live data status is marked per target.

**Rules:**
- Public marketing pages and public earnings/press only.
- No scraping behind logins, no private comp data.
- Cite source URL + date_checked in every CSV row.
- Flag "incomplete advertised pricing" when fees/APR/comp grids are partial.

**Date prepared:** 2026-05-28

## Segment A — Retail Lenders (compete for LO recruits + borrower share)

### 1. Rocket Mortgage / Rocket Companies
- **Public pitch (1 line):** America's largest mortgage lender with end-to-end digital origination.
- **Why we research:** Sets the consumer-brand bar. Their tech narrative is what brokers must counter.
- **What to look for:** Latest AI/automation messaging on rocket.com careers + investor relations; LO recruiting pitch (or absence of one).
- **Live data status:** Pending live collection.

### 2. UWM (as wholesale channel, since we are broker-channel)
- **Public pitch (1 line):** #1 wholesale lender supporting independent brokers.
- **Why we research:** They define broker-channel narrative. Their TPO portal features set expectations for what brokers expect from any platform.
- **What to look for:** Broker tools, TPO marketing, AI/automation rollouts, broker-recruitment messaging at UWM events.
- **Live data status:** Pending live collection.

### 3. loanDepot
- **Public pitch (1 line):** Tech-forward retail lender with mello tech stack.
- **Why we research:** mello stack messaging is what their LOs hear weekly — useful contrast for our integrated platform pitch.
- **What to look for:** mello positioning, LO recruiting pages, AI/automation features advertised.
- **Live data status:** Pending live collection.

### 4. Guaranteed Rate Affinity / Rate
- **Public pitch (1 line):** Retail lender pushing rapid digital application + branch network.
- **Why we research:** Aggressive LO recruiting and rebrand cycles — useful benchmark for recruiting deck.
- **What to look for:** Recruiting page, LO-tier benefits language, AI tools advertised to recruits.
- **Live data status:** Pending live collection.

### 5. CrossCountry Mortgage
- **Public pitch (1 line):** Large retail lender with strong LO-coaching culture.
- **Why we research:** They invest heavily in LO development — direct competitor for any LO weighing a move.
- **What to look for:** Training/coaching pages, AI/tech stack pages, public production rankings.
- **Live data status:** Pending live collection.

### 6. Movement Mortgage
- **Public pitch (1 line):** Purpose-driven retail lender with strong culture story.
- **Why we research:** Their culture pitch is sticky — useful to understand the emotional pull they have on LOs we want.

## Capture Workflow

1. For each target, fill one row in `competitor_intel_capture_template.csv` first (public pitch, segment, pricing, summaries, source URL, date_checked).
2. If the competitor owns a platform, also fill one row in `platform_feature_comparison_template.csv`.
3. Mark `strongest_pitch_in_1_line` and `weakest_pitch_in_1_line` from a Loan Factory recruiting-conversation perspective.
4. Fill `where_LF_differentiates` using the differentiation anchors in `RESEARCH_INDEX.md`.
5. Route batches of 5 completed rows back to strategy lead weekly.

## Differentiation Anchors (paste-ready for every "where_LF_differentiates" field)

- Integrated platform: training + coaching + AI + community + lender escalation in one place.
- AI Twin per role (LO / Team Leader / Recruiter / Marketing) tuned to Loan Factory tone + compliance posture.
- Internal-only safety posture — never an external chatbot.
- Member tier ladder $249 LO Mastery -> $449 Alliance bundles AI + coaching cadence.
- FaceGram internal community closes the loop on AI Advantage + coaching feedback.
