## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### Goal
Configure the Loan Factory website and QM Pricer so the loan officer's website is presentable, trackable, and useful for lead conversion.

### Who This Is For
Loan Factory loan officers who want their Loan Factory website and quote/pricer experience to support online lead generation.

### Exact Navigation Path
Loan Factory portal > Website Settings > Website tab or QM Pricer settings tab.

## Website Settings Steps
1. Open Website Settings from the left menu.
2. Confirm whether you want to use your own domain name.
3. Confirm the website URL slug.
4. Decide whether the website URL should show on the email signature.
5. Confirm whether to join and promote the Best Price Guarantee if eligible and approved.
6. Decide whether email and phone number should show on the website.
7. Add a website video URL if you have one.
8. Upload or replace the background/profile image if needed.
9. Upload a thumbnail link preview logo for social and messaging previews.
10. Review and update your bio.
11. Add custom scripts only when you know what they are for, such as Google tracking tags or remarketing scripts.
12. Add custom metadata only when needed.
13. Confirm working schedule and all-day availability settings.
14. Review custom header menu and loan product sections.

## QM Pricer Setup Steps
1. Open the **QM Pricer settings** tab.
2. Confirm whether contact information is required to get a price.
3. Add the contact information disclaimer if needed.
4. Review the general disclaimer. Use clear language explaining rate accuracy, volatility, and that pricing is not a loan approval.
5. Choose which lenders to show, same as company or preferred lenders.
6. Review default quote form values including loan type, purpose, loan amount, property value, ZIP code, occupancy, and FICO range.
7. Confirm FHA upfront MIP and VA funding fee options.
8. Review rate display settings, including whether rates show around the best rate or within a specific range.
9. Choose how many rates to display.
10. Decide whether to show lenders by name, logo, or not at all.
11. Review whether compensation details are displayed.
12. Set any BPC minimum/maximum fields only if appropriate.
13. Confirm whether to show rates in .125.
14. Decide whether to display create alert, apply, qualify, and view fees buttons.
15. Review which loan terms display on login and non-login quote views.

## Important Jeremy Compliance Note
Jeremy's business uses lender-paid compensation only. If a screenshot shows borrower-paid compensation selected, do not copy that as a default setting for Jeremy without specific approval. Training should say to verify compensation settings with leadership or company policy.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaving the website bio outdated.
- Forgetting to test the public website URL after changes.
- Adding multiple tracking scripts without understanding what they do.
- Showing pricing buttons without a clear follow-up plan.
- Copying QM Pricer compensation settings without checking company policy.
- Displaying too many loan terms, which can confuse consumers.

## How to Know It Worked
The public Loan Factory website should show the updated profile, video, bio, and visibility options after the cache delay. The QM Pricer should show rate/quote output according to the selected display rules.
