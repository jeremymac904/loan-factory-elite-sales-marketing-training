Here are the step-by-step instructions from the transcript on how to set up Website Visitor Audiences in Google Ads:

**Step 1: Navigate to the Audience Manager**  
In the left navigation menu, locate and click **Tools**. Navigate to the **Shared Library** section, then access the **Audience Manager** feature.

**Step 2: Add a New Segment**  
Click the **plus button (+)** to add a new segment. Select **Website Visitors** from the menu.

**Step 3: Name Your Segment**  
Enter your desired audience name in the **Segment Name** field (e.g., *Website Visitors loanfactory.com/quote*).

**Step 4: Choose the Visitor Type**  
Choose **Visitors of Web Pages** from the options. Click **Select Customer Type**, then select **All Customers** - since you're building a general website visitor audience, not limiting to specific customer types.

**Step 5: Set the Retargeting Time Frame**  
Set your retargeting time frame - for example, **365 days** for long-term nurturing.

**Step 6: Refine the Targeting**  
Select **Refine Action** to customize your website visitor targeting. Enter the **website URL** you wish to track visitor data from.

**Step 7: Prefill the Segment**  
Choose to **prefill the segment with people from the past 30 days**. Then enter any additional details about the segment.

**Step 8: Save the Segment**  
To finalize, click **Create Segment** to save your audience settings.

**Step 9: Verify Your Configuration**  
Double-check your audience segment configuration to ensure accuracy. Click the **vertical ellipsis (⋮)** to view settings, then select **Edit List**. After reviewing, click **Save** if changes were made, or **Cancel** if no changes are needed.
