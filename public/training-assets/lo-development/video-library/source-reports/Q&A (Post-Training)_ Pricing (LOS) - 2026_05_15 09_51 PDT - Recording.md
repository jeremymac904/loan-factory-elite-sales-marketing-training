### **4\. Questions this Video Answers**

* **LO Question:** Why isn't Pylon 100 basis points better on every scenario?  
  * **Answer:** 100 bps is a marketing figure based on specific "perfect" scenarios; while Pylon is generally better, actual savings vary by loan-to-value (LTV) and program.  
  * **Timestamp:** 03:55  
  * **FAQ:** Yes | **Automation:** No  
* **LO Question:** Can I do an FHA loan through Pylon?  
  * **Answer:** No. Pylon currently only supports Conventional and Jumbo loans.  
  * **Timestamp:** 28:57  
  * **FAQ:** Yes | **Automation:** Yes (Topic: Pylon Eligibility)  
* **LO Question:** How do I disclose a Buyer's Broker Fee if the seller isn't paying it?  
  * **Answer:** Place it in Section H (Other Fees). Avoid Section C as it's not a required loan cost.  
  * **Timestamp:** 47:58  
  * **FAQ:** Yes | **Automation:** No
