### **1\. Quick Video Recap**

This recording provides a comprehensive guide on avoiding common mistakes in the 1003 loan application process. It covers critical checkpoints from initial application to underwriting, explains the roles of internal support staff (processors, loan coordinators, and corporate coaches), and demonstrates how to use Loan Factory’s AI and marketplace tools.

* **Who it helps**: Primarily new loan officers (LOs) learning the system and experienced LOs looking to reduce deal fallout.  
* **Platform Location**: This belongs in the **1003 and Application Setup** and **Survival Guide** categories.

### **4\. Questions This Video Answers**

* **LO Question**: Where do I find the company AI tool?  
  * **Answer**: Go to Resources \> Important Company Links \> Loan Factory AI.  
  * **Timestamp**: 00:56.  
  * **FAQ/Automation**: Yes.  
* **LO Question**: Why is my DU approval failing even if the ratios look okay?  
  * **Answer**: Check if the appraised value field was updated to match the purchase price. Also, consider changing the term from 30 to 25 years.  
  * **Timestamp**: 04:46 / 05:19.  
  * **FAQ/Automation**: Yes.  
* **LO Question**: Can I credit closing costs on a borrower-paid wholesale loan?  
  * **Answer**: No, lender credits for closing costs are generally for lender-paid compensation plans only.  
  * **Timestamp**: 13:59.  
  * **FAQ/Automation**: Yes.
