### **1\. Quick Video Recap**

This recording provides a deep dive into the financial mechanics of loan origination at Loan Factory. It covers the critical differences between wholesale (lender-paid vs. borrower-paid) and correspondent compensation, detailed calculation examples for loan estimates, and how to configure personal pricing settings in the MOS system. It is primarily for **new loan officers (LOs)** to understand their pay and **experienced LOs** transitioning to the correspondent channel. This belongs in the **Compensation and Fees** and **Pricing Loans** categories of the LO Development platform.
