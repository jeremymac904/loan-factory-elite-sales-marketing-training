### **1\. Quick Video Recap**

This recording is the final installment of the loan life cycle onboarding series. It covers the transition from a prospect to an active loan, the submission process to lenders, working with disclosure specialists, managing the processing phase, and finally requesting commission. This is a foundational resource for new loan officers and belongs in the **First File Survival Guide** or **Compliance and Process Reminders** categories.

### **3\. Best Clips to Cut**

#### **Clip 1: Fixing the 99% Completion Error**

* **Start/End:** 01:25 – 03:40  
* **Ideal Length:** 2 minutes  
* **Problem Solved:** LOs can't convert a prospect because it's stuck at 99%.  
* **Audience:** New LOs  
* **Thumbnail Title:** FIXED: 99% Complete Error  
* **Description:** Learn the "Save and Qualify" trick on the last page of the 1003 to hit 100% completion and enable the "Convert to Loan" button.  
* **Edits:** Screen zoom on the "Save and Qualify" button; captions for the step-by-step instructions.

#### **Clip 2: TRID Compliance: When to Add the Address**

* **Start/End:** 21:50 – 24:38  
* **Ideal Length:** 2:45  
* **Problem Solved:** Avoiding accidental TRID triggers that lead to compliance violations.  
* **Audience:** New LOs, Training Academy  
* **Thumbnail Title:** Don't Trigger TRID Early  
* **Description:** Important warning on why you should wait until 100% ready before adding a property address in the system.  
* **Edits:** Add a "Warning" title card; highlight the 24-48 hour window mentioned.

#### **Clip 3: The Proper Way to Lock a Rate**

* **Start/End:** 54:33 – 55:54  
* **Ideal Length:** 1:20  
* **Problem Solved:** Rate locks not showing in the system or failing compliance.  
* **Audience:** All LOs  
* **Thumbnail Title:** How to Lock Rates Correctly  
* **Description:** The essential two-step process: Lock with the lender first, then update the workflow in TERA.  
* **Edits:** Screen zooms on the "Workflow" tab and "Rate Locked" checkbox.
