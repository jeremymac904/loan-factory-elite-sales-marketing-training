# FaceGram Post Style Guide

**Format standard for all Loan Factory FaceGram posts.** FaceGram is the **internal community feed** for approved Loan Factory users (member wins, tips, training nudges, AI tips, celebrations).
**Inherits:** `LOAN_FACTORY_BRAND_OUTPUT_RULES.md`. Read that first.
**Internal Loan Factory community — approved Loan Factory users only. Not public social media.**

---

## 1. What FaceGram is (and isn't)
- It's the **internal** feed inside the Loan Factory LO Development platform — peer-to-peer, supportive, momentum-building.
- It is **not** public Facebook/Instagram and **not** consumer marketing. Do not apply consumer NMLS/APR disclosure formatting here (that's for external mortgage marketing). Keep it internal and team-facing.
- If a post would ever leave the platform or face borrowers/agents, it must instead follow the marketing compliance rules (NMLS 320841, EHL, rate+APR, etc.) and route through compliance.

## 2. Post anatomy
```
[Hook line — 1 short line that stops the scroll]
[1–3 short lines of value or the win]
[1 line CTA or question to drive replies]
#LoanFactory #<program tag>   (light, optional)
```
- **Length:** 2–5 short lines. Front-load the point.
- **Emojis:** 0–3, purposeful (✅ 🔥 📈 🎯). Never a wall of emojis.
- **Tone:** energetic, encouraging, broker-first, real — but **no hype, no income claims, no manifestation language.**

## 3. Post types by program
- **LO Mastery — member post:** celebrate execution/wins, weekly-rhythm nudge, scorecard shout-out, "book three calls today" prompt.
- **Alliance — post:** premium/leadership tone, top-producer wins, mastermind takeaway, advanced challenge.
- **Sales & Marketing 101–601 — training post:** light lesson nugget from a session; frame as free training, kept separate from paid coaching.
- **AI Advantage — AI tip post:** one quick AI/tool/workflow win an LO can use today (a prompt, a HeyGen tip, a NotebookLM trick).

## 4. Branding
- Orange `#F26A1F` accents in any attached graphic; black/white base; logo on graphics.
- Attribution optional/light ("— Jeremy / LO Development") for official posts; member-voice posts can be casual.
- "Internal Loan Factory use only" is implied by the channel — only add the label if the post is exported as a template.

## 5. Voice rules (from brand rules)
- Practical, specific, doable today. Celebrate the action, not just the result.
- Use **LO**, **TERA**, **Netlify**, **approved Loan Factory users**. No Apex Advisor.
- No income guarantees, no "borrow to join," no manifestation, no hype.
- Keep Sales & Marketing visibly free + separate from paid coaching.

## 6. Production note
Output 1–3 paste-ready post variants per request. If a graphic is wanted, spec it to the brand palette and pair with the relevant image skill. Run the Pre-Send Brand Checklist.
