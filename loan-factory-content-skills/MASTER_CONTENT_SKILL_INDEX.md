## 3. What each skill must NEVER do

**All skills (global law):** no hype · no income/production guarantees · no manifestation language · no "borrow money to join" · no Apex Advisor / Apex Advisor Pro · always use **LO** (not ELO), **TERA** (not MOSO), **Netlify** (not Vercel), **approved Loan Factory users** (not public). Anything consumer/agent-facing follows the Marketing Compliance Rules (NMLS 320841, EHL, rate+APR, sample scenario, draft footer, route through compliance).

| Skill | Especially never |
|---|---|
| **LO Mastery** | Never promise outcomes/income; never blur with free Sales & Marketing; coaches never run team meetings, handle billing, or approve member marketing without compliance routing. |
| **Loan Factory Alliance** | Never let "premium" become hype; never expose private production data (leaderboards anonymized unless opt-in); never disparage non-members. |
| **Sales & Marketing 101–601** | **Never** present it as a paid coaching program or money-gated; keep it free, downplayed, and separate from LO Mastery/Alliance. |
| **AI Advantage** | Never overstate AI capability or invent APIs/integrations; never skip the NMLS footer / human approval gates on HeyGen; never assume advanced skill (beginner floor). |

## 4. How to keep PDFs, slides, emails, and scripts consistent

1. **Load the law first.** Every job starts with `LOAN_FACTORY_BRAND_OUTPUT_RULES.md`, then the matching style guide, then the skill.
2. **One palette everywhere:** Orange `#F26A1F` (accent/CTA) · Black `#111111`/`#000000` · White `#FFFFFF` · greys `#E5E7EB` / `#5F6368`. No off-brand colors. System sans font. Logo placed per guide.
3. **Same required spine on every output:** title → subtitle → audience line → "Internal Loan Factory use only" (where appropriate) → concise sections → **one** strong CTA → 2–4 next steps → attribution (Loan Factory — LO Development · Jeremy McDonald, The Mortgage Mentor).
4. **Same vocabulary:** correct program names + prices; LO / TERA / Netlify / approved Loan Factory users; paid vs. free kept distinct.
5. **Same voice discipline per program:** LO Mastery = practical/supportive/execution-focused · Alliance = premium/strategic/leadership · Sales & Marketing = Jeremy broker-first/direct · AI Advantage = forward-thinking/beginner-friendly/execution-first. All four: no hype.
6. **Reuse the grounded blocks** (LO Weekly Activity Tracker fields, 24 Production Certifications, AI Suite, 6-part AI session template, HeyGen Hook→Rehook→Sections→CTA) so a scorecard, deck, and email about the same topic line up.
7. **Run the Pre-Send Brand Checklist** (in the brand rules file) before delivering anything. Deliver paste-ready.
