# HeyGen Video Script Style Guide

**Format standard for all Loan Factory HeyGen avatar video scripts** (coaching videos, lesson intros, AI Advantage videos, market updates).
**Inherits:** `LOAN_FACTORY_BRAND_OUTPUT_RULES.md`. Read that first.
**Internal Loan Factory use only.**

---

## 1. Script container (always output this shape)
```
TITLE: <video title>
PROGRAM: <LO Mastery | Alliance | Sales & Marketing 101–601 | AI Advantage>
AUDIENCE: Approved Loan Factory users — <segment>
LENGTH: <0:30 / 1:00 / 1:30 target>
PERSONA: <see §3>

[HOOK — 0:00–0:08]
<spoken script>

[BODY — main points]
<spoken script, broken into short beats>

[CTA + NEXT STEP]
<spoken script>

ON-SCREEN TEXT: <2–5 short orange/white lower-thirds>
B-ROLL / VISUAL CUES: <optional>
```

## 2. Writing for text-to-speech (HeyGen)
- Write the way Jeremy **talks**: short sentences, contractions, one idea per line.
- Add line breaks between beats so the avatar paces naturally.
- Spell tricky terms phonetically in brackets for the voice engine, e.g., TERA (Tair-uh), Fannie Mae, DSCR (D-S-C-R), HELOC (HEE-lock).
- Avoid long parentheticals, em-dash pileups, and unpronounceable symbols. Write "percent" not "%", "and" not "&".
- Numbers: write out short ones ("three"), keep figures clear ("a 30-year fixed").

## 3. Persona selection (Jeremy's AI twin)
- **Inside Man** — education / how-to (default for lessons, LO Mastery, AI Advantage).
- **Coach** — guidance, encouragement, accountability (LO Mastery / Alliance).
- **Wolf** — market updates, Fed/rate commentary, momentum (Alliance / market posts).
Pick one per video and keep the energy consistent.

## 4. Structure & timing
- **Hook (first 8 seconds):** name the problem or the win. No throat-clearing.
- **Body:** 1–3 tight points, each a few spoken lines.
- **CTA + next step:** one clear action ("Open your tracker and book three calls today").
- Target 30–90 seconds unless told otherwise.

## 5. On-screen text & branding
- Lower-thirds and key words in **orange `#F26A1F`** on dark, or white on dark; keep to a few words.
- Loan Factory logo bug where supported. "Internal Loan Factory use only" on internal training cuts.

## 6. Voice & compliance
- Practical, broker-first, confident, no hype.
- No income guarantees, no rate promises, no manifestation language, no "borrow to join."
- Educational framing for anything rate/program-specific. Use LO / TERA / Netlify; never Apex Advisor.
