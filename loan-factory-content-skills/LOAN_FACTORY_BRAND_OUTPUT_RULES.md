## 1. The Loan Factory Visual System

These values are pulled from the live Loan Factory LO Development site. Use them exactly.

### Color palette (orange / black / white)

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
|------|------|-----|-----|
| Primary accent | Loan Factory Orange | `#F26A1F` | CTAs, key headings, highlights, rules, bullets, data emphasis |
| Primary dark | Near-Black | `#111111` | Body text, dark backgrounds, headlines |
| Deep background | Black | `#000000` | Hero/cover backgrounds, slide backgrounds |
| Secondary dark | Charcoal | `#2B2B2B` | Cards, panels, secondary backgrounds on dark |
| Base light | White | `#FFFFFF` | Page/slide backgrounds, text on dark |
| Divider / border | Light Grey | `#E5E7EB` | Lines, table borders, card outlines |
| Muted text | Grey | `#5F6368` | Captions, labels, footnotes, metadata |

**Rules of thumb**
- Orange is an **accent**, never a wall of color. Use it for emphasis, CTAs, and one or two hero elements per page/slide — not full backgrounds of body text.
- Default document = white background, near-black text, orange accents.
- Default "hero"/cover = black background, white text, orange accent.
- Never introduce off-brand colors (no blues, greens, purples) except neutral greys above.

### Typography
- **Font family:** system sans-serif stack — `ui-sans-serif, system-ui, -apple-system, "Segoe UI", Roboto, "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif`. When a tool needs a named font, use **Arial / Helvetica Neue** (PDF, slides) or **Roboto** as the closest web-safe equivalent.
- **Headlines:** bold, tight, sentence case or title case. Large and confident.
- **Body:** regular weight, generous line height, short paragraphs.
- Never use decorative, script, or condensed display fonts.

### Logo
- File: `loan-factory-logo-transparent.png` (wordmark "LOAN FACTORY" with orange accent letters).
- Live URL: `https://loan-factory-elite-sales-marketing-tr.netlify.app/images/brand/loan-factory-logo-transparent.png`
- Place top-left on documents, top-left or centered on slide covers. Keep clear space around it. Use the transparent version on white or black. Never stretch, recolor, or add effects.

### Buttons / CTAs (for slides, emails, web-style blocks)
- Orange fill `#F26A1F`, white text, **8px** corner radius, bold label, generous padding.
- One primary CTA per view. Secondary actions = outline/ghost style (border `#E5E7EB`, dark text).

### Tone of the visual system
Clean, modern, confident, professional. Card-based layouts, clear hierarchy, lots of white (or black) space. Think "operator's dashboard," not "hype funnel."

## 4. Global Language Rules (non-negotiable)

**Always use these terms:**
- **LO** (loan officer) — **never "ELO."**
- **TERA** — **never "MOSO."**
- **Netlify** (hosting/deploy references) — **never "Vercel."**
- **Approved Loan Factory users** — **never "public users," "the public," "customers,"** or "anyone." Audience is internal.
- **Loan Factory** branding and naming throughout.

**Never use / never mention:**
- **Apex Advisor** or **Apex Advisor Pro** — do not reference at all.
- **Hype language** ("explode," "10x overnight," "secret," "guru," "crush it" as a promise).
- **Income guarantees** or earnings claims ("you will make $X," "guaranteed income," "replace your salary").
- **Manifestation / mindset-magic language** ("manifest," "law of attraction," "abundance will flow," "speak it into existence").
- **"Borrow money to join"** language or any suggestion to take on debt / finance enrollment.
- Calling **Sales & Marketing** a paid coaching program.

**Always be:** practical, mortgage-specific, broker-first, execution-driven, supportive, clear. Speak to working loan officers about real activities (calls, applications, follow-up, scripts, pipeline, partners, content).

**Broker-first / compliance posture:** Loan Factory is broker-positive. Keep claims factual and educational. No rate promises, no guaranteed approvals, no fair-lending-sensitive targeting language. When in doubt, soften to educational framing.

## 5. Required Structure for Every Output

Every PDF, slide deck, email, handout, scorecard, worksheet, agenda, script, and post must include (adapted to format):

1. **Clear title** — what this is, in plain words.
2. **Subtitle** — one line of context or the outcome.
3. **Audience line** — e.g., "For: Approved Loan Factory users — LO Mastery members."
4. **Internal use label** — "Internal Loan Factory use only" where appropriate (PDFs, decks, handouts, scorecards, trackers, agendas, workflow/handoff docs). Light or omitted on FaceGram posts and short social copy.
5. **Loan Factory language** — program names and terms per Sections 3–4.
6. **Consistent orange/black/white visual system** — per Section 1.
7. **Concise sections** — scannable headings, short blocks, bullets over walls of text.
8. **Strong call-to-action** — one clear primary action.
9. **Next steps** — 2–4 concrete, doable actions.
10. **Attribution where appropriate** — "Loan Factory — LO Development" and/or "Jeremy McDonald — The Mortgage Mentor."
