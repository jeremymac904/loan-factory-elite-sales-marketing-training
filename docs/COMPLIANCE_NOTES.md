# Compliance Notes

This document is a training reference for Loan Factory team members. It is not legal advice. Compliance review is required for any borrower facing, Realtor facing, or public use.

## The four pillars

1. Regulation Z. Triggering terms in advertising.
2. SAFE Act. NMLS ID display.
3. RESPA Section 8. No things of value for referrals.
4. FFIEC interagency social media guidance. Social media is regulated like any other channel.

## Regulation Z (12 C.F.R. § 1026.24)

1. Definition. A Reg Z advertisement is a commercial message in any medium that promotes a credit transaction.
2. Triggering terms. A specific down payment, a payment period, a specific payment amount, or a specific finance charge.
3. When a triggering term appears, the message must also disclose the amount or percentage of any down payment, the terms of repayment over the full term including any balloon, and the APR using that term.
4. The interest rate may be listed but not more conspicuously than the APR.
5. Live conversation has more flexibility, but the same redirect rule applies. Anchor on the borrower's plan, not a teaser headline.

## SAFE Act NMLS ID

1. Display the NMLS ID prominently on advertising and public communications.
2. Add it to social profiles, captions where credit is promoted, email signatures, and text signatures.
3. Include the Loan Factory NMLS where required.

## RESPA Section 8

1. Settlement service referrals cannot be exchanged for things of value.
2. Marketing Services Agreements can become unlawful if facts and circumstances show payment for referrals or payments in excess of fair market value.
3. Co marketing is permissible only when both parties pay pro rata for joint advertising and no thing of value is exchanged for referrals.
4. No gift cards, paid subscriptions, paid event tickets, or paid services that benefit the referring party.
5. Any new MSA requires corporate approval.

## FFIEC interagency social media guidance

1. Existing consumer protection and compliance laws and regulations apply to social media activities as they would to other channels.
2. Captions and on screen text are part of the advertisement.
3. Direct messages quoting specific terms are advertisements.

## Loan Factory specific policies

1. No teaser rates in any borrower facing artifact.
2. No production guarantees in any training, certification, or marketing artifact.
3. No invented competitor facts. Lead with what Loan Factory does.
4. Internal training only. Do not publish this portal externally.
5. Past client outreach about specific rate or payment terms is a Reg Z advertisement.

## Safe content decision tree

1. Does the post mention a specific rate, payment, finance charge, down payment dollar amount, or term in months. If yes, treat as a Reg Z advertisement.
2. Is the NMLS ID present.
3. Is the Equal Housing logo present where required.
4. Are there any superlatives (best rate, guaranteed approval, lowest in town). If yes, rewrite.
5. Are there any direct comparisons to a named competitor with unverified facts. If yes, rewrite.
