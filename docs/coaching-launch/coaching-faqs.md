## Platform

### What is LO Mastery

LO Mastery is a Loan Factory training and development platform for loan officers. It brings the Sales and Marketing 101-601, live coaching, certifications, summits, and the Alliance Mastermind community into one operating system.

### What is the Sales and Marketing 101-601

The Sales and Marketing 101-601 is the foundation course inside the platform. Six modules, 101 through 601, that install one behavior a week with scripts, AI prompts, roleplays, audio training, and a weekly tracker.

### Who is LO Mastery for

Loan officers who want a real growth path. New loan officers use it to build a foundation. Experienced producers use it to plug leaks, sharpen partner work, and add live coaching.

## Membership

### What are the two tiers

LO Mastery at $249 per month is the foundation tier. Loan Factory Alliance at $449 per month adds monthly live mastermind calls, direct coaching access, advanced TERA workflows, priority content requests, the Alliance Summit, and the Alliance Mastermind community.

### Can I switch tiers

Yes. You can move from LO Mastery to Loan Factory Alliance at any time. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### Can I cancel

Yes. Membership is month to month. You can cancel any time.

## Certifications

### What certifications are available

There are four coaching certifications. ACLO (Certified Mortgage Advisor), LO Mastery TERA Power User, LO Mastery Marketing Pro, and Loan Factory Alliance Graduate.

### Do certifications guarantee production or income

No. Certifications recognize completion of training and assessments. They are not a guarantee of production, income, or business results.

### How do I display a certification

Add it to your email signature, your LinkedIn, your business card, and your borrower welcome packet. Any borrower facing, Realtor facing, or public claim still goes through Loan Factory compliance review.

## TERA and tools

### What is TERA

TERA is Loan Factory's loan origination software, point of sale, and CRM platform. LO Mastery includes a TERA module and advanced workflows. Loan Factory Alliance adds advanced TERA workflow coaching.

### What if I do not use TERA

The non-TERA modules still apply. The TERA Power User certification requires TERA use.
