## Email 1 — Day 0. Welcome

**Subject.** You are in. Here is your week one move.
**Preheader.** LO Mastery starts the day you decide to take this seriously.

Welcome to LO Mastery.

I built this platform because most loan officers do not need another course. They need a platform. A place that pulls the training, the live coaching, the certifications, and the community into one operating system.

Your week one move is simple.

1. Open 101 Foundation. That is the start of the Sales and Marketing 101-601.

## Email 2 — Day 2. Use the tools

**Subject.** The tools that make week one easy
**Preheader.** Scripts, prompts, roleplays, audio. Pull them in this order.

Hey {{first_name}},

Two days in. Here is the order to pull tools so you do not get lost.

1. Script Library. Pull the first call script for borrower discovery.
2. AI Prompt Library. Use the call prep prompt before your next live call.
3. Roleplay Library. Run one short roleplay before you do anything live.

## Email 3 — Day 7. First week review

**Subject.** Your first week as an LO Mastery
**Preheader.** Review, lock the habit, set week two.

You just finished week one. Here is what to do today.

1. Pull your Weekly Tracker and look at the numbers. Conversations. Partner touches. Past client touches.
2. Open the Sales and Marketing 101-601. Mark 101 Foundation as complete.
3. Open 201 Borrower Conversion. That is your next module.

If something cracked this week — a deal, a partner, a lead — bring it to me. Pro members can bring it to the next mastermind call. LO Mastery members can bring it to the next live Q and A on the calendar.

This is the rhythm. One module a week. One behavior at a time.

Stay sharp.

— Jeremy
