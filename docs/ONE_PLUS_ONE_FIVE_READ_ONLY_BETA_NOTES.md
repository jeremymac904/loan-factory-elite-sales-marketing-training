# 1+1+1=5 Read-Only Beta Notes

## Beta Purpose

The 1+1+1=5 module helps Team Leaders plan weekly growth lanes without creating live campaigns.

It answers:

1. Who are we targeting?
2. What do they care about?
3. What should we say?
4. Where should we market?
5. What should the LO do this week?
6. How do we measure if it worked?

## Watch Items

- Keep the module simple enough for Team Leaders to skim.
- Avoid dense research-library pages.
- Do not use persona/community notes to include or exclude borrowers.
- Do not imply different treatment, pricing, approval, underwriting, eligibility, or product availability.
- Do not use protected-class traits for paid ad targeting.
