# Curriculum Map

The six week training series in one document. Use this as the single reference for what each module promises, who it is for, and what behavior change to expect.

## 101 Foundation. The Loan Factory Operating System

1. Core promise. By Friday, you have a daily rhythm that creates conversations.
2. Audience. Brand new LO or experienced LO who wants a simple weekly system.
3. Main lesson. Sales math, daily activity standard, weekly rhythm, broker value prop.
4. Assignment. 25+ logged conversations, GBP or LinkedIn updated, 3 personalized past client texts, one note on what slowed you down.
5. Behavior change. LO finishes the week with at least 25 logged conversations.

## 201 Borrower Conversion. Win the First Call

1. Core promise. You convert more pre quals into pre approvals and more pre approvals into apps.
2. Audience. Any LO with 30 or more days of LO experience.
3. Main lesson. First call structure, talk to listen ratio target 43:57, plan first language, rate and fee redirects, dated next step.
4. Assignment. Three recorded first calls with consent, self score against the rubric, one written recap.
5. Behavior change. LO talk to listen ratio moves toward the 43:57 golden ratio. Question quality improves week over week.

## 301 Referral Partner Growth. Build the Partner Engine

1. Core promise. You add one productive referral partner per week.
2. Audience. Any LO with a stable borrower process.
3. Main lesson. Realtor strategy, partner value prop, first meeting structure, listing agent call, RESPA dos and don'ts.
4. Assignment. 25 priority partners listed with public research, 10 personalized outreaches, one new partner meeting booked, one listing agent call.
5. Behavior change. LO has 5 named priority partners and at least one active deal sourced from one of them.

## 401 Content and Marketing. Local Authority, Compliant Content

1. Core promise. You build a credible, findable, broker positive presence.
2. Audience. Any LO ready to be visible.
3. Main lesson. Personal brand pillar, five hooks, 60 second video, Google Business Profile build, newsletter, compliance safe content decision tree.
4. Assignment. Three short videos posted, GBP fully built, monthly newsletter drafted, every piece run through the safe content decision tree.
5. Behavior change. LO has a real digital footprint a borrower or Realtor can find.

## 501 Pipeline and Sales Systems. Run the Engine Like a Pro

1. Core promise. You stop losing deals to mess.
2. Audience. Producer or experienced LO.
3. Main lesson. Pipeline hygiene, weekly status updates, past client database by tenure, weekly pipeline review, AI assisted follow up.
4. Assignment. Pipeline cleaned and segmented, 20 personalized past client touches, one full Friday pipeline review.
5. Behavior change. LO retention moves from the industry baseline toward 40%+ over 24 months.

## 601 Execution System. Build Your Niche, Own Your System

1. Core promise. You graduate with a written 12 week plan and a working AI stack.
2. Audience. Producer, team leader, or corporate coach.
3. Main lesson. Niche strategy, 90 day campaign plan, weekly rhythm by role, scorecard review, AI workflow, certification.
4. Assignment. 12 week niche plan, AI workflow checklist, one page scorecard, partner roster of five.
5. Behavior change. LO operates with a written plan and a measurable scorecard.

## Certification requirements

1. All assignments through 101 to 601 completed.
2. Two recorded calls scored above the first call rubric threshold.
3. One 12 week niche plan submitted.
4. One AI workflow demonstrated live to your coach.
5. Weekly tracker submitted at least four times during the series.

## Compliance flags by module

1. 101. No specific rates, payments, fees in outbound. NMLS ID on every borrower facing message.
2. 201. No teaser rates. Live conversation flexibility, written communication must follow Reg Z.
3. 301. RESPA Section 8. No things of value for referrals. Co marketing pro rata only.
4. 401. Reg Z triggering terms list. NMLS ID. Equal Housing logo. No superlatives. No unverified competitor comparisons.
5. 501. Past client outreach about specific rates or payments triggers Reg Z disclosures.
6. 601. All niche marketing reviewed before publish.
