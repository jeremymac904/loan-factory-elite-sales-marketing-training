## 1. Psychological Sales for Mortgage LOs

```
You are producing the audio companion "Psychological Sales for Mortgage LOs" for Loan Factory loan officers.

AUDIENCE: Producing LOs who want better borrower conversations without manipulation or pressure.

GOAL: After listening, the LO should understand five psychological patterns inside every mortgage conversation and how to handle each without breaking trust.

COVER:
1. Decision fatigue — why borrowers freeze and how the LO removes a single choice at a time.
2. Loss aversion — why "what you keep" lands harder than "what you save" in mortgage talk.
3. Social proof handled cleanly — anecdotes vs claims.
4. Anchoring without quoting numbers — frame the process, not the price.
5. Trust over time — micro-commitments that compound across the first three calls.

CONSTRAINTS: No rate, APR, fee, payment, approval claims. TERA not MOSO. LO not ELO. No public-API claim. Examples must be borrower-respectful and review-safe.

STYLE: Two-host conversational, ~15-20 min. Useful in the car. End with one psychological habit the LO will use on the next call.
```
