# Slide 4: Professional advisor, not rate order taker

**Headline:** Help, do not pitch.

**Body bullets:**
- Order takers quote and hope. Advisors plan and guide.
- Lead with goals, timeline, and the borrower's plan
- Numbers come after the plan, not before
- Compliance reminder. Live conversation flexibility is not permission to put specific rates, payments, or fees into writing without disclosures

**Visual direction:** Split panel. Left side silver chip labeled `Order taker`. Right side orange chip labeled `Advisor`. Clean and minimal.

**Speaker note:** Call out the trap. Quoting a rate to a stranger is the worst version of this job. Show the advisor frame.

**Pause point:** None.

## Gamma design instructions

1. Use bold dark hero slides. Navy and charcoal only.
2. Use orange accent lines and orange dots. Loan Factory orange.
3. Use minimal text. Five lines per slide maximum.
4. Use large typography for the headline.
5. Use a premium sales academy feel. Not corporate boring. Not cartoon.
6. Use icons sparingly. Tracker, chain, partners. Keep them line icons.
7. Use no cartoon elements.
8. Use no stock photos unless they feel premium and business focused. Houses, real LO desks, real mortgage moments only.
9. Use clean backgrounds or subtle gradient panels.
10. Logo lockup on title and final slide. Elite Sales and Marketing Training logo top. Loan Factory logo equal weight below or beside it. Never tiny.

## Compliance guardrails embedded in the deck

1. No rates, payments, APR, down payment amounts, or specific fee examples.
2. No claim of free processing.
3. No claim of an open Loan Factory API.
4. No production guarantees.
5. No bashing banks or retail lenders.
6. TERA is only used to describe Loan Factory's LOS, POS, and CRM. AI references use Gemini Gem AI Twin or AI Coaching Assistant.
7. Internal training only. Do not distribute outside Loan Factory without compliance review.
