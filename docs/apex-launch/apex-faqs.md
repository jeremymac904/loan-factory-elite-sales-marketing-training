## Platform

### What is Apex Advisor

Apex Advisor is a Loan Factory training and development platform for loan officers. It brings the Apex Advisor Track, live coaching, certifications, summits, and the Apex Mastermind community into one operating system.

### What is the Apex Advisor Track

The Apex Advisor Track is the foundation course inside the platform. Six modules, 101 through 601, that install one behavior a week with scripts, AI prompts, roleplays, audio training, and a weekly tracker.

### Who is Apex Advisor for

Loan officers who want a real growth path. New loan officers use it to build a foundation. Experienced producers use it to plug leaks, sharpen partner work, and add live coaching.

## Membership

### What are the two tiers

Apex Advisor at $249 per month is the foundation tier. Apex Advisor Pro at $449 per month adds monthly live mastermind calls, direct coaching access, advanced TERA workflows, priority content requests, the Apex Summit, and the Apex Mastermind community.

### Can I switch tiers

Yes. You can move from Apex Advisor to Apex Advisor Pro at any time. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### Can I cancel

Yes. Membership is month to month. You can cancel any time.

## Certifications

### What certifications are available

There are four Apex certifications. ACLO (Apex Certified Loan Officer), Apex TERA Power User, Apex Marketing Pro, and Apex Advisor Pro Graduate.

### Do certifications guarantee production or income

No. Certifications recognize completion of training and assessments. They are not a guarantee of production, income, or business results.

### How do I display a certification

Add it to your email signature, your LinkedIn, your business card, and your borrower welcome packet. Any borrower facing, Realtor facing, or public claim still goes through Loan Factory compliance review.

## TERA and tools

### What is TERA

TERA is Loan Factory's loan origination software, point of sale, and CRM platform. Apex Advisor includes a TERA module and advanced workflows. Apex Advisor Pro adds advanced TERA workflow coaching.

### What if I do not use TERA

The non-TERA modules still apply. The TERA Power User certification requires TERA use.
