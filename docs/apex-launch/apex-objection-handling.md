# Apex Advisor — Objection Handling

**Compliance review flag.** This is mortgage marketing content. Route through Loan Factory compliance before using in customer facing conversations or written replies.

**Audience.** Loan officers considering Apex Advisor or Apex Advisor Pro.
**Format.** Common objection, the short answer in my voice.

---

## Objection 1. The price is too high

The price is the work. $249 per month for Apex Advisor or $449 per month for Apex Advisor Pro is not the cost of a course. It is the cost of a platform that pulls the training, the live coaching, the certifications, and the community into one operating system.

If you close one extra deal a year because of what you learn here, the math is finished.

This is not a guarantee. It is a math reality. You decide if you are willing to do the work.

---

## Objection 2. I already have a coach

Good. Keep your coach. Apex Advisor is a platform, not a one-on-one coach.

The platform gives you the curriculum, the scripts, the AI prompts, the roleplays, the tracker, and the community. The Pro tier adds live mastermind calls. Your existing coach can plug into the system, not compete with it.

---

## Objection 3. I do not have time

Apex Advisor is built around one behavior a week, not a thirty hour curriculum. The Apex Advisor Track is six modules. One module a week. Each module installs one behavior with a script, a prompt, a roleplay, and a tracker line.

If you do not have time for one behavior a week, the issue is not Apex Advisor. The issue is the calendar.

---

## Objection 4. I am brand new. Is this for me

Yes. The Beginner Path inside the Apex Advisor Track is built for brand new loan officers. You will start with 101 Foundation and stay in sequence. You will not need to figure out where to start.

---

## Objection 5. I am already producing. Will I get value

Yes, if you are honest about your gaps. Most experienced LOs are leaking pipeline somewhere. The platform gives you a clean way to find the leak and fix it. Apex Advisor Pro adds live mastermind calls so you can bring real deals and real partners to coaching.

---

## Objection 6. Why not just buy a course

Because a course ends. A platform compounds.

Apex Advisor includes the course (the Apex Advisor Track), the certifications, the live calls, the resource libraries, and the community. You get the curriculum and the system that surrounds it.

---

## Objection 7. What is the difference between Apex Advisor and Apex Advisor Pro

Apex Advisor is $249 per month. You get the Apex Advisor Track, the resource libraries, the member area, and eligibility for the ACLO certification.

Apex Advisor Pro is $449 per month. You get everything in Apex Advisor plus monthly live mastermind calls, direct coaching access, advanced TERA workflows, priority content requests, the Apex Summit, and the Apex Mastermind community.

Pick the one that matches the work you are willing to do.
